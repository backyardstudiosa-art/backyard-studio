About

A creative sanctuary for memory, meaning, and becoming.

Backyard Studio is rooted in the belief that stories deserve gentleness. Our work blends reflection and design so people can process life honestly and preserve it beautifully.

From New Horizons reflections to guided journaling tools, everything is crafted as an evergreen companion — not a trend cycle.
