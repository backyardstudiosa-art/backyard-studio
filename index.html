Who I Am

Backyard Studio is an extension of who I am and what I believe in: that creativity and healing are not separate paths, but ones that often walk hand in hand.

I blend thoughtful design, storytelling, and gentle therapeutic practices to help individuals find balance, clarity, and meaning in both personal and professional lives. My work is rooted in the belief that creativity can restore, ground, and guide us back to ourselves when life feels fragmented.

Backyard Studio was born from quiet seasons of healing and rediscovery. It is a place where calm meets creativity, where inspiration flows at a natural rhythm, and where growth is allowed to unfold without force or rush.

This is an invitation to create, to reflect, and to move forward with clarity and grace.
