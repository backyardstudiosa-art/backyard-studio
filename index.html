Backyard Studio

Where your stories are held gently.

Digital Scrapbooks, Memory Books & Faith-Centred Journaling in South Africa

A Johannesburg-based creative studio where storytelling, design, and personal growth meet.

Backyard Studio creates personalised digital scrapbooks, memory books, illustrated keepsakes, and guided journals for individuals and brands across South Africa.

Inspired by the rhythm of the ocean — steady, grounding, renewing — this is where your story is preserved with intention.

Why Backyard Studio Exists

Backyard Studio was created for the tender parts of life: for the memories you don’t want to forget, for the thoughts that feel too layered for captions, and for the seasons that deserve to be processed, not just survived.

Journal Pillar

New Horizons

Recurring reflections for thoughtful living.

New Horizons is the journal pillar of Backyard Studio. A space for layered writing, spiritual reflection, and quiet encouragement. These entries are not trends. They are timeless notes for the soul. Each reflection is searchable, evergreen, and written to be revisited, like pages you return to when you need grounding.
