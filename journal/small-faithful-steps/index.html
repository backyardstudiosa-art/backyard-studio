Small, Faithful Steps

Big transformations often begin with one quiet yes repeated over time.

← Back to Journal

February 14, 2026 · Faith · Growth

I used to wait for certainty before beginning. Now I am learning to move with trust instead.

One page written. One honest conversation. One gentle boundary. These things are not dramatic, but they make a life sturdy.

Faith is not always a mountain-top feeling. Sometimes it is choosing the next faithful step when no applause is in the room.
