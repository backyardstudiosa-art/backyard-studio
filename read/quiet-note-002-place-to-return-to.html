It’s Not Just Consistency

It’s not only about doing something every day,
or never missing a beat.

Sometimes it’s about arriving again.

About returning to the page, the practice, the prayer, the work
without shame for the space in between.

Consistency says, keep going.
But grace says, you’re allowed to come back.

Life doesn’t always move in straight lines.
There are pauses, detours, quiet seasons where nothing looks productive
but everything is becoming.

Picking up where you left off doesn’t mean you failed at consistency.
It means you trusted yourself enough to return.

To begin again, not from the beginning,
but from experience.

From what you already learned.
From who you already became.

Showing up is not always loud or perfect or daily.
Sometimes it’s simply choosing not to abandon yourself.

And that, too, counts.
